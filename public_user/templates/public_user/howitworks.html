how it works

Read through the steps to understand the process
If you have more questions, please book free consultation with us!

1.

We will have initial consultation, where we find out more about your project and your requirements. We will discuss images, colors, your audience, SEO, Google Adsense, Google Analytics... We will advise on how to create your Privacy Policy,Terms of Use, where to host your new website and where to purchase your domain name.

2.

When you have all the information you need, you can purchase product you are interested in, and we will start creating your site.

3.

We will start working on your project by initially drawing wireframes, and sending it to you for your approval.

click on any image to enlarge it

4.

We will then apply changes you want.

5.

We start creating your site.

6.

You will have live link to development site, so you can see as your site is being developed.

7.

When the site is ready, after your approval, we will transfer your domain name to your site, and you can start using your site the way you want!
